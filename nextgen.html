I would like to study for this course because I have always enjoyed playing and creating video games from a young age. Which has helped me to improve my skills and creativity in different styles of games production. I have then been able to explore every aspect that goes into creating games at a professional level during my time at college. This course would be a good fit for me as it will help me work towards my goals for the future. All these skills will then help me with my future goal of being a solo indie developer. My qualifications and studies has helped me prepare for this course as it has helped me understand how much detail goes into designing, coding and creating games overall. I have learnt how to create discord bots for servers as well as creating websites to showcase work that I have completed or worked on throughout my game development journey so far.
